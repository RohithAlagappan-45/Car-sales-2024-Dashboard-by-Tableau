# 🚗 Car Sales 2024 Dashboard (Tableau)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Car Sales Dashboard for 2024**, built using Tableau to analyze sales performance across different car brands, models, and body types. The dashboard provides valuable insights into market trends, customer preferences, and overall sales distribution.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze total car sales by body type and brand
* Identify top-performing car models in 2024
* Understand monthly sales trends and seasonality
* Provide insights to support data-driven business decisions

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Tableau (Data Visualization)
* Excel (Data Source)

## 📊 Key Features

* Sales distribution by **body type (SUV, Hatchback, Sedan, MUV)**
* Brand-wise sales comparison
* Top 25 car models visualization
* Monthly sales trend analysis
* Interactive filters for dynamic exploration

## 💡 Insights

* SUVs dominate the market with the highest sales volume
* Maruti leads in overall brand sales, followed by Mahindra and Kia
* Popular models include Swift, Baleno, Creta, and Nexon
* Sales show variation across months, indicating seasonal demand patterns

## 🚀 Future Improvements

* Add predictive analytics for future sales forecasting
* Integrate real-time or updated sales data
* Enhance dashboard UI/UX for better user experience
* Include customer segmentation and pricing analysis
